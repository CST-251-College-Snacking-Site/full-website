    Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

    Ingredients

    • Buttermilk Bread
    • Chunky Peanut Butter
    • Blackberry Jam

    How to cook

    This is all you can really afford.

    1. Step 1: Spread peanut butter evenly onto one slice of bread using a knife.

    2. Step 2: Spread jelly or jam evenly onto the other slice of bread.

    3. Step 3: Press the two slices of bread together.
