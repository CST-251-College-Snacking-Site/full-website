    Hamburger Sliders

    Ingredients

    • 2lbs ground beef (75% lean)
    • A whole buncha seasonings
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • King's Hawiian Rolls
    • Spray Oil
    • Melted Butter
    • Parsley

    How to cook

    1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.

    2. Step 2: In a large bowl, season the meat to your liking.

    3. Step 3: In a deep oven safe pan, squish the ground beef until it creates a even layer that covers the whole bottom

    4. Step 4: Cook the ground beef for 25 minutes. Remove from oven

    5. Step 5: Remove the meat from tray and drain the excess oil. Spray the pan with the cooking oil.

    6. Step 6: Cut the rolls in half like the where to be made into a hamburger. Line the bottom of the tray as with the bottom half of the roll. Put the meat on top of the bottom buns. Then layer with cheese

    7. Step 7: Put back into the oven to cook until the cheese is melted. Remove from oven and put the top bun. Brush the melted butter and parsley over the top bun. Put back in the oven for another 8 mins. Remove from oven.

    8. Step 8: Enjoy
