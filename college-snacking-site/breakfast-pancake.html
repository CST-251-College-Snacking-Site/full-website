    Chocolate Chip Pancakes

    Ingredients

    • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
    • 3 teaspoons baking powder
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 1/4 cups milk
    • 1 egg
    • Butter
    • Mini Chocolate chips

    How to cook

    1. Step 1: Preheat a griddle or skillet until it is hot.

    2. Step 2: In a large bowl, combine all of the ingredients except the butter.

    3. Step 3: Melt a quarter tablespoon of butter on the skillet and pour the mixture.

    4. Step 4: Cook the pancake on each side for about a minute or two. Remove from heat.

    5. Step 5: Garnish with a bit more butter, syrup and more pancakes.

    6. Step 6: Enjoy!
