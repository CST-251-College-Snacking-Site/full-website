    Chicken and Dumplings (Slow Cooker)

    Ingredients

    1 Large Onion

    1 Can Cream of Celery

    1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup

    1 Tbsp parsley

    1 tsp Poultry Seasoning

    Black Pepper (to taste)

    Garlic Powder (to taste)

    4 Skinless Chicken Breast

    2 Cups Chicken Broth

    2 Cupus of Frozen Vegetables

    1 Can of Refigerated Biscuits

    How to cook

    Step 1: Add onion to 6qt crock pot and top with chicken breast.

    Step 2: In a small bowl, combine cream of celery, cream of chicken soup, parsley, poultry seasoning, pepper and garlic powder.

    Step 3: Spread mixture from step 2 over chicken breast and top with chicken broth and cook on high for five hours.

    Step 4: Roughly one hour before serving, (after the four hour mark), roll each biscuit until thin and flat. Cut them into four strips. Addthe vegetables to slow cooker and stir. Add biscuit strips on top. Replace lid as quickly as possible.

    Before Serving

    Step 5: Remove chicken breast and slightly shred. Add back to the slow cooker and stir. Let cook for additional 10 minutes.
