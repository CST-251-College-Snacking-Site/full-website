    Busicuts and Gravy

    Ingredients

    1 Can Refigerated Buttermilk Bisucits

    1 package of Pork Sausage (roughly 9 ounces)

    1/4 Cup of All Purpose Flour

    2 1/2 Cups of Milk

    Salt and Black Peper (to taste)

    How to cook

    Step 1:Bake Biscuits according to directions on package.

    Step 2:Cook sausage in a large skillet over medium heat for 5- 6 units or until throughly cooked, stirring frequently.

    Step 3:Stir in flour. Gradually add milk and cook until mixture comes to a boil and thickens.

    Step 4: REduce heat to a medium low, simmer for two minutes, stirring soncatnly. Season with salt and pepper.

    Step 5: Split Biscuits in half, place halves on a plate and top with gravy.
