    Burrito

    Ingredients

    • 5 Eggs
    • Mission Tortillas
    • Pork Churizo
    • Salt & Pepper
    • Cheese
    • A potato
    • Cooking Oil

    How to cook

    1. Step 1: Heat up a skillet and add oil.

    2. Step 2: Start frying the pork churizo until it starts to brown. Remove.

    3. Step 3: Pill the potato and cut up into small peices. Fry until brown and soft. Remove

    4. Step 4: In a small bowl beat the eggs then pour into a hot skillet with oil. Before the egg fully cooks, add the pork churizo and potatos.

    5. Step 5: Remove from heat and start heating the tortillas. Add the cheese.

    6. Step 6: Roll the burritos.

    7. Step 7: Enjoy
