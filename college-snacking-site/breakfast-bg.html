    Turkey Omelette

    Ingredients

    • Turkey Deli Meat
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • 1 Egg
    • Vegetable Oil
    • Shredded Cheese

    How to cook

    1. Step 1: Take 3 slices of your turkey deli meat and rip them to small pieces.

    2. Step 2: Take the 1 Egg and crack it open in a small bowl. Use a fork or whisk to beat the egg.

    3. Step 3: Place a pan on a stove and set in a medium heat. Add your vegetable oil in the pan and wait unitl both are hot.

    4. Step 4: Once the pan is hot enough, add in your ripped deli meat. Use some tongs to toss them around for about a minute or two.

    5. Step 5: After those two minutes, pour the beaten egg on top of the turkey then quickly season it with salt and pepper.

    6. Step 6: When you see the outer part of the egg fully cooked, take a spatula and flip the whole thing over. While the other side is cooking spread your shredded cheese on top.

    7. Step 7:Fold the whole thing over itself and cook for a few more seconds for the cheese to melt. Take it out and it's ready to be served with toast and some coffee.
