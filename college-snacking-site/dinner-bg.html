    Fried Rice

    Ingredients

    • White Rice
    • Spam
    • 2 Eggs
    • Cummin Powder
    • Garlic Powder
    • Cayanne Pepper
    • Minced Onion
    • Frozen Vegetables
    • Knorr's Chicken Bouillon
    • Knorr's Liquid Seasoning
    • Vegetable Oil

    How to cook

    1. Step 1: Take about 1-2 cups of white rice and place it to the bowl of a rice cooker. Wash and rinse it with water 3 times. After the 3rd time add more water to the bowl and place in your rice cooker. Set it to cook and wait for 30-40 minutes.

    2. Step 2: While your rice is cooking, take your spam and cut it up into small cubes. On a non-stick pot, set it on a medium heat and cook your spam with a spatula. Cook for about 5 minutes or until the spam has a nice red color to it. Place it on a plate until the rice is ready.

    3. Step 3: Crack your 2 eggs into a small bowl and beat them with a fork or whisk. Go back to the pot and add a little bit of vegetable oil. Cook the eggs in the pot and season it with a little bit of salt. Once your scrambled eggs are done place them on the same plate with your spam.

    4. Step 4: If the rice is ready, use the same pot and pour in more vegetable oil set it to a medium heat. Scoop your cooked rice and put in the pot. Use your spatula to break up the rice clumps and start frying it.

    5. Step 5: Once the rice is all broken up, lower the heat to medium-low and now would be the time to sprinkle in your spices in this order: gralic powder, cummin powder, cayanne pepper, minced onion, a tablespoon of the chicken bouillon, and a couple drops of liquid seasoning. Once that is done, mix everything together.

    6. Step 6: After you mix all the spices with the rice, add in your frozen vegetbales. Keep mixing them around until the vegetables are thawed out.

    7. Step 7: Throw in the spam and eggs you cooked earlier and add them in to the fried rice party. Mix them around for a minute or so and it's ready to serve.
